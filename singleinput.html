Do You Want to Play a Game?

All you have to do is pick a number, pick a game, and get a surprise!

Ping Pong

A simple game of call and response. You give me a number, and I answer back according to some rules:

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 will become "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 will become "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by 15 will become "pingpong"

Sieve of Eratosthenes

The Sieve of Eratosthenes is a method to discover all the primes below a given number. For more details, see here.

Roman Numerals

We've all seen Roman Numerals, but the details of how to produce them can be confusing. Convert any number below 4000 to its Roman equivalent!
